Welcome to Task Scheduler
Task Scheduler is a simple application helps you to order your urgent tasks based on their priorities and decides which one is the most important for you.
How it works?
You will enter your tasks in pairs based on the following: the task that you must finish before the second one and the second task that is less important than the first then click to (Try) to get a sequence of tasks ordered from the most important or urgent for you to the least one

For Example: if you have 3 tasks and the third one is more important than the second you enter 3-2 and if the second is more important than the first you enter 2-1 so if you entered: 3-2 in the first line then 2-1 in the second line the sequence will be ordered according to its importance for you (3,2,1) task 3 is the most important then second task then task one has the least importance. you can enter strings as well. Try it yourself !
